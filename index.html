Triangle Classification

The basic elements of any triangle comprise its sides as well as its angles.

By considering their sides, triangles can be classified as follows:

  1. Equilateral: All three sides are equal.

    2. Example of an Equilateral triangle

  2. Isosceles: Only two sides are equal.

    3. Example of an Isosceles triangle

  3. Scalene: All the three sides are different.

    4. Example of a Scalene triangle
